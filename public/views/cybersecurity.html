Naija Cyber Shield: Complete Cybersecurity Course

Protect yourself, your business, and Nigeria's digital future with our comprehensive cybersecurity course. Learn how to defend against cyber threats, secure networks, and understand the cybersecurity landscape with a focus on African contexts and challenges.

This course is designed for Nigerians and Africans who want to build cybersecurity skills to protect against local and global threats. From understanding common "Yahoo Yahoo" scams to enterprise-level security, we cover it all.

Wetin You Go Learn? (What Will You Learn?)

Understand cybersecurity fundamentals and Naija-specific threats
Protect against common Nigerian cyber scams and fraud
Secure networks, systems, and mobile devices
Learn ethical hacking to find vulnerabilities before criminals do
Implement data protection strategies for businesses
Understand cybersecurity laws and regulations in Nigeria
Develop incident response plans for organizations
Build a cybersecurity career with international certifications

Wetin You Need (Requirements)

  • Willingness to Learn & Open Mind
  • Computer/Laptop (Windows or Mac)
  • Internet Connection
  • No prior cybersecurity experience required

Who This Course For?

  • IT Professionals looking to specialize in cybersecurity
  • Business Owners wanting to protect their companies
  • Students interested in high-demand cybersecurity careers
  • Anyone concerned about online safety in Nigeria
