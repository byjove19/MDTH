Video Editing Masterclass

Master professional video editing with Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects in this comprehensive course. Learn industry-standard techniques for creating stunning visuals, compelling narratives, and professional-grade video content.

This course is designed for beginners and intermediate editors who want to build a strong foundation in video editing and create portfolio-worthy projects that will impress clients and employers alike.

What Will You Learn?

Master Adobe Premiere Pro interface and workflow
Create professional transitions and effects
Color correction and grading techniques
Audio editing and sound design fundamentals
Motion graphics and visual effects with After Effects
Export settings for different platforms
Workflow optimization and keyboard shortcuts
Build a professional video editing portfolio

Requirements

  • A computer with Adobe Creative Cloud (free trial available)

  • No prior video editing experience required

Who is this course for?

About Alex

Alex Thompson began his career as a freelance video editor while studying film at NYU. His work quickly gained recognition, leading to collaborations with major production houses and streaming platforms.

In 2018, Alex founded his own online education platform with the mission to make professional video editing skills accessible to everyone. His courses are known for their practical approach and real-world applications.

When not teaching or editing, Alex enjoys creating documentary films and mentoring young filmmakers in underprivileged communities.
